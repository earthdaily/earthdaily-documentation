Today environmental compliance services play a relevant role in the search for the preservation of the environment, in addition to protecting companies from financial and reputational risks. This module enables Brazilian customers to quickly check whether or not a given geometry (field, farm, policy or other) complies with environmental and national legislation. It is used to confirm that there is no overlap between the geometry and protected areas such as native land, permanent protection areas, legal reserves, etc.

## ðŸ“¥ Access Field Analysis Details

### Generate Compliance Analysis for a Selected Field

Once you have a global view of your field of interest, you'll be able to select the field you would like to analyze. Fields colored in red or green have already been analyzed. Click on a grey field to perform analysis on it.

<br/>

![compliance](../images/COMPLIANCE_3.png)
<br/>

A panel will open on the right. Click on the **GENERATE** button on the bottom right of the panel. A loader will appear and your report will be generated in few seconds.

<br/>

![compliance](../images/COMPLIANCE_4.png)
<br/>

### View Compliance Results

Once a compliance analysis has been done on the field you can access all its detail through this right panel.
It is divided in three sections:

- The first one allows you to get global information on your geometry (when you performed the analysis, where it is located, in which biome, whether or not is it part of legal Amazon...)

![compliance](../images/COMPLIANCE_5.png)
<br/>

- The second one allows you to get detail of each compliance item (the layers for which we check the overlap), with the associated detail (% of overlap if any). You can click on the display icon to see the features of the layer that intersect your geometry.

![compliance](../images/COMPLIANCE_6.png)
<br/>

- The third allows you to downloaded the PDF of your report with all previous information generated.

![compliance](../images/COMPLIANCE_7.png)
