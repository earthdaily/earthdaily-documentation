# Monitoring - Field



## Entity Selection

When viewing the Monitoring Module you first need to select the Entity(s) you would like to view.  In Field Monitoring, the selection panel will by default display all Growers/Farms which have an In Season Field.


![EarthdailyApp](../images/FES_images/Mon_Grower_List_Map.jpg)

Click on the Filter Icon to open otions which will allow you to make selections shorten the Grower/Farm based on In Season or Sowing Date range, Crop Type, Usage and/or Irrigation Status.



![EarthdailyApp](../images/FES_images/Mon_Grower_List_Filter.jpg)


Select one or many Growers/Farms and see all the fields in either a Map or Grid view.  To toggle between the two views click on the map or list icon in the top right of the screen.

![EarthdailyApp](../images/FES_images/Mon_Grower_Farm_Selection_Map.jpg)


![EarthdailyApp](../images/FES_images/Mon_Grower_Farm_Selection_Table.jpg)


## Time Series Viewer

Click on a Field and it will open the Time Series Viewer panel.  Click on the Years on the right side of the panel to add more curves.  Click on the date calendars at the top of the panel to adjust the Start and End Dates of the Time Series.


![EarthdailyApp](../images/FES_images/Mon_Time_Series.jpg)


### Select Analytic

Click on the Select Analytic icon to choose from the available analytics for your Time Series.  Field level analytics include Vegetation, Precipitation and Temprature.

![EarthdailyApp](../images/FES_images/Mon_Time_Series_Select_Analytic.jpg)


### Add Time Series

Add additional Time Series views to the panel by clicking the Add button in the bottom right of the panel and selecting the Analytic you wish to view.

![EarthdailyApp](../images/FES_images/Mon_Two_Time_Series.jpg)
