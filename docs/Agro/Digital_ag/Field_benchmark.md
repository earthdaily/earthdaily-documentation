### Potential Score (Historical & In-Season)

This analytic evaluates how a field performs during the growing season compared to its historical performance for the same crop. Using satellite imagery time series, it provides a simple, scalable indicator leveraging vegetation development as a proxy for yield potential. With minimal inputs required, the Potential Score offers a consistent, repeatable way to benchmark field productivity across large portfolios.
