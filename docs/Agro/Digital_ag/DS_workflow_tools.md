# Turning Insights into Action

Turn insights into action with **Satellite-Derived Management Zones (SAMZ)** that help you understand field variability over time and under different weather conditions, guiding targeted operations. When it is time to execute, generate **Rx Maps** that support precise input application, optimizing both cost and performance. Whether you’re planting, fertilizing, or applying any number of products, we support your applications with zoning precision and the ability to choose product quantities based on composition and unit selection.

Identify emerging issues at a glance with our **HotSpot Processor**, which highlights areas that are changing and may require immediate attention—ensuring you never miss a developing problem and can act fast.

# Custom Insights for Your Precise Need

With 35 years of experience, we’ve learned a great deal from our customers. Through that learning, we’ve developed specific analytics, reports, and exports tailored to their business needs—adding value for their operations and their customers alike. Some of these include:

- **Harvest Date Prediction** processor, facilitating the timing of your harvest operations  
- **PDF Reports** with your logos and branding, telling a clear story you can easily distribute  
- **Exports** in Shapefiles or CSVs of your requested datasets  
- **John Deere DataSync** — seamlessly send maps to the John Deere Information System  
- **Notifications** configured to your preferences, adjustable anytime  
- **Email campaigns** with vital, up-to-date information and insights
