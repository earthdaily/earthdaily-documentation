### Webinar and Video Series

#### Weekly Webinars
[Click here to access a webinar recording](#)

Join our Crop Analyst for weekly interactive webinars where EarthDaily will review Global Crop Production estimates and the Vegetation and Weather data which are driving these estimates.

**Reasons to join our webinars:**

- **Insights** - Gain valuable insights into crop conditions.
- **Ahead of the market** - Stay ahead of the market with information at the beginning of the week which can confirm or challenge the market noise.
- **Production figures** - Estimated production of the main grains in the world's main producers.

Webinar recordings are available after they have concluded so you can watch them at a time more convenient for your workflow.

#### Weekly Videos

Weekly short form videos are available to provide updates on the current production conditions and forecasts.

**Weekly Videos available include:**

- **Global Crop Production Highlights**
  - Weekly updates on key production areas where conditions are most different when compared to previous seasons.
  - Watch this video to stay aware of major stories of the growing season.

- **Global crop specific videos** for Corn, Soybeans, Wheat, Rapeseed/Canola, and Barley
  - Weekly updates on all the production areas of the target crop
  - Highlights when our forecasts or conditions have changed recently or we differ from USDA

#### All Crops Global Report
