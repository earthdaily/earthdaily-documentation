### 2024 Russian Winter Wheat Case Study

**Extremely wet start of the cycle**

![Wet start conditions](../../assets/agro/commodities_intelligence/Picture5.png)

**Record dry conditions in the following months**

![Dry conditions](../../assets/agro/commodities_intelligence/Picture6.png)

**Strong late cold snap in May**

![May cold snap](../../assets/agro/commodities_intelligence/Picture7.png)

**Warm waves at the end of the cycle negatively rushing the grain maturation**

![End of cycle warm conditions](../../assets/agro/commodities_intelligence/Picture8.png)
